About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Supreetha, a data analyst with over five years of experience at Infosys and Accenture. I love working with data, whether it's building dashboards in Power BI and Tableau, writing SQL queries, or using Python to clean up messy datasets. I like to simplify things. If a report or a dashboard can't tell the story clearly, it's not done yet. I have an eye for the small details that make a big difference in how data gets understood and used.

Over the years I've also enjoyed mentoring junior analysts, leading requirements sessions with stakeholders, and helping teams stay aligned on what the data is really saying. I'm always curious and open to learning, and I genuinely believe that the best work happens when everyone on the team feels heard and supported.

Outside of work, I love travelling and exploring new places, reading, gardening, and trying out new recipes in the kitchen. Staying curious in all parts of life is something I carry into my work every day.
